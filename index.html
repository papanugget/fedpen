Federation Pension Bureau, Inc. was organized to provide comprehensive, technical and professional assistance to clients in the formulation, design and maintenance of Employee Benefits Plans. For the last 20 years it has been owned by Philip Stashin and David Cahn.

The staff of Federation Pension Bureau, Inc. includes experts with many years of practical experience in all phases of actuarial consulting, plan design and administration. This staff is available to assist our clients not only in the design of the benefit program, but also the formulation of all necessary administrative procedures.

We are fully familiar with the contracts and services offered by insurance companies, banks, service and investment organizations whose products and services are commonly employed by Pension, Profit-Sharing and Welfare/Medical Plans. Accordingly, we are able to advise and appraise our clients of these services and their suitability for the plan under consideration, and to provide sufficient information for the client to make an intelligent evaluation of these products and services. We are completely independent in such matters, since we are not affiliated with any insurance company, bank, investment firm or service organization. In our dealings with these firms, our only purpose is to serve the interests of the client.

Since its inception, Federation Pension Bureau, Inc. has specialized in the development of employee benefit plans of all types for Corporations, Labor Organizations, and Trustees of Jointly-Administered funds.

At the present time, our firm provides actuarial, consulting and administrative services to more than 100 different Funds, Labor Organizations and Employer groups. The plans vary in size, type of benefits, industry and location.
